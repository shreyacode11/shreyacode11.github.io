About

I grew up curious about what happens behind the screen — how a click turns into a change someone else sees, instantly, on the other side of a room or a country. That curiosity is basically still my whole personality. I'm an Information Science student at Global Academy of Technology, Bengaluru, and somewhere along the way "how does this work" turned into "let me build one."

I've spent the last year bouncing between an internship portal, a gaming venue's website, and a production codebase — each one teaching me a different way software actually gets used by real people, not just tested by me at 1am. Outside of code, I run media for VTU Fest and I'm the person who says yes to a 24-hour hackathon on a Friday night.

What I'm chasing next: a Software Development Engineer role where I can keep learning by shipping, not just studying.
